Saudi Arabian National Football Team

History and Achievements

Early History

The Saudi Arabian national football team was established in 1956. The team is known for its strong performances in international football and is one of the most successful teams in Asia. Saudi Arabia joined FIFA in 1956 and participated in various tournaments ever since.

First World Cup Appearance

The Saudi Arabian national team made its first appearance in the FIFA World Cup in 1994. This was a historic moment for Saudi football as the team qualified for the tournament in the United States. Saudi Arabia reached the round of 16, which was considered a major achievement at the time.

Key Achievements

  • Four-time AFC Asian Cup winners (1984, 1988, 1996, 2024)
  • Five-time FIFA World Cup appearances (1994, 1998, 2002, 2006, 2018)
  • Consistently ranked among the top teams in Asia

Recent Developments

In recent years, Saudi Arabia has continued to invest in football development, with a focus on improving youth training and infrastructure. The team has shown progress and continues to be a competitive force in international football.
